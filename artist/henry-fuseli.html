约翰·亨利希·菲斯利是一位德国-瑞士裔的英国画家、制图员及艺术家。

Henry Fuseli was a Swiss painter, draughtsman, and writer on art who spent much of his career in Britain. Many of his successful works depict supernatural experiences, such as The Nightmare.
