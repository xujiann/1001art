Alexandros of Antioch

古希腊

1 件作品 / works

安提俄克的亚历山德罗斯，古希腊化时期雕塑家，被认为是《米洛的维纳斯》的作者，约活跃于公元前二世纪末。

Alexandros of Antioch was a Hellenistic Greek sculptor credited with the Venus de Milo, active around the late 2nd century BC.
