阿格桑德罗斯等

Agesander and others

古希腊

1 件作品 / works

阿格桑德罗斯与另两位罗德岛雕塑家，被古罗马作家老普林尼记为《拉奥孔》群雕的作者，作品以强烈的痛苦表情与扭动的体态震撼后世。

Agesander and two fellow sculptors of Rhodes are named by Pliny the Elder as creators of the Laocoön group, whose anguished expressions and writhing forms astonished later ages.
