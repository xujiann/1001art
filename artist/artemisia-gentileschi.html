阿尔泰米西娅·真蒂莱斯基

Artemisia Gentileschi

1593–1653 · 教皇国

24 件作品 / works

阿尔泰米西娅·洛米·真蒂莱斯基，意大利巴洛克画家。在那个女画家十分少见的年代，她率先创作了历史及宗教画。

Artemisia Lomi Gentileschi was an Italian Baroque painter. Gentileschi is considered among the most accomplished 17th-century artists, initially working in the style of Caravaggio.
