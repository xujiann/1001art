安托万·华托

Antoine Watteau

1684–1721 · 法国

1 件作品 / works

让-安托万·华托，法国洛可可绘画的开创者。首创「雅宴」题材，以轻盈的笔触描绘游园男女的优雅风姿与淡淡愁绪。

Jean-Antoine Watteau was the originator of the French Rococo, inventing the fête galante genre that depicts elegant outdoor gatherings with a light touch and a note of gentle melancholy.
