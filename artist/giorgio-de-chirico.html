乔治·德·基里科

Giorgio de Chirico

1888–1978 · 意大利

1 件作品 / works

乔治·德·基里科，出生于希腊的意大利画家，形而上画派创始人。以空旷广场、拉长的阴影与古典拱廊营造出静谧而不安的梦境，启发了超现实主义。

Giorgio de Chirico was an Italian painter born in Greece and founder of the Metaphysical school; his empty piazzas, long shadows and classical arcades evoke a still, unsettling dreamscape that inspired Surrealism.
