亚历山大·罗斯林

Alexander Roslin

1718–1793 · 瑞典

1 件作品 / works

亚历山大·罗斯林，瑞典肖像画家，活跃于巴黎等欧洲宫廷，为众多贵族绘制了华美精致的肖像。

Alexander Roslin was a Swedish painter who worked in Scania, Bayreuth, Paris, Italy, Warsaw and St. Petersburg, primarily for members of aristocratic families.
