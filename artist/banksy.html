班克斯

Banksy

英国

1 件作品 / works

班克斯，身份成谜的英国街头艺术家与政治活动者。以模板喷绘在公共空间创作，作品充满反战、反消费主义与社会批判的黑色幽默。

Banksy is an anonymous England-based street artist and activist; working in stencilled spray paint in public spaces, the work is charged with anti-war, anti-consumerist and satirical black humour.
