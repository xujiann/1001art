贾斯培·琼斯，美国当代艺术家，波普与新达达的先驱。以国旗、靶子、数字等熟悉符号入画，探讨图像、符号与观看之间的关系。

Jasper Johns is an American contemporary artist and a pioneer of Pop and Neo-Dada; painting familiar signs—flags, targets, numbers—he probes the relationship between image, symbol and seeing.
