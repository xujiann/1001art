王希孟

Wang Ximeng

1096–1119 · 中国

1 件作品 / works

王希孟，北宋天才画家，约十八岁绘成青绿山水长卷《千里江山图》，画成后不久即早逝，是中国山水画史上的传奇。

Wang Ximeng was a prodigy of the Northern Song who painted the monumental blue-green landscape A Thousand Li of Rivers and Mountains around the age of eighteen and died soon after—a legend of Chinese landscape painting.
