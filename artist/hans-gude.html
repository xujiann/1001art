汉斯·古德

Hans Gude

1814–1876 · 挪威

1 件作品 / works

汉斯·古德，挪威浪漫民族主义风景画家，杜塞尔多夫画派的代表人物，常与蒂德曼合作，代表作《哈当厄尔的婚礼行舟》。

Hans Gude was a Norwegian Romantic-nationalist landscape painter and a leading figure of the Düsseldorf school, best known for Bridal Voyage in Hardanger, painted with Adolph Tidemand.
