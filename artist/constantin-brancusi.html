康斯坦丁·布朗库西

Constantin Brâncuși

1876–1957 · 罗马尼亚

1 件作品 / works

康斯坦丁·布朗库西，罗马尼亚裔法国雕塑家，现代主义雕塑的先驱。以《空间之鸟》《吻》等作品将形体高度提炼、抽象，追求事物的本质。

Constantin Brâncuși was a Romanian-born French sculptor and pioneer of modernism who, in works such as Bird in Space and The Kiss, pared form down to a radical, essential abstraction.
