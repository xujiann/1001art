图特摩斯

Thutmose

古埃及

1 件作品 / works

图特摩斯，古埃及第十八王朝的宫廷雕塑家，约活跃于公元前十四世纪；著名的《娜芙蒂蒂胸像》即出土于他位于阿马尔纳的工坊。

Thutmose was an ancient Egyptian court sculptor of the 18th Dynasty, active around the 14th century BC; the famous Bust of Nefertiti was found in his workshop at Amarna.
