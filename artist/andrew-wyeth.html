安德鲁·怀斯

Andrew Wyeth

1917–2009 · 美国

1 件作品 / works

安德鲁·怀斯，美国现实主义画家，以蛋彩与水彩描绘宾夕法尼亚与缅因的乡间。代表作《克里斯蒂娜的世界》以细腻笔触传达孤寂与怀想。

Andrew Wyeth was an American Realist painter who portrayed rural Pennsylvania and Maine in tempera and watercolour; his best-known work, Christina's World, conveys solitude and longing through meticulous detail.
