波留克列特斯

Polykleitos

古希腊

1 件作品 / works

波留克列特斯，古希腊古典时期雕塑家，活跃于公元前五世纪。提出人体比例的「法则」，代表作《持矛者》成为理想人体的典范。

Polykleitos was a Classical Greek sculptor of the 5th century BC who formulated the Canon of ideal human proportion, exemplified by his Doryphoros (Spear-Bearer).
