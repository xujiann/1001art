弗里达·卡罗

Frida Kahlo

1907–1954 · 墨西哥

2 件作品 / works

弗里达·卡罗，墨西哥画家，以强烈自传色彩的自画像闻名。作品融合墨西哥民间艺术与超现实意象，直面身体的苦痛、身份与女性经验。

Frida Kahlo was a Mexican painter known for her intensely autobiographical self-portraits, blending Mexican folk art with surreal imagery to confront physical pain, identity and female experience.
