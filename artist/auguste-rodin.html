奥古斯特·罗丹

Auguste Rodin

1840–1917 · 法国

3 件作品 / works

奥古斯特·罗丹，法国雕塑家，被视为现代雕塑的奠基者。以《思想者》《加莱义民》《吻》等作品著称，善于以富有张力的形体表现人的情感与生命力。

Auguste Rodin was a French sculptor generally regarded as the founder of modern sculpture, celebrated for works such as The Thinker, The Burghers of Calais and The Kiss, in which he modelled the human form with unprecedented emotional and physical intensity.
