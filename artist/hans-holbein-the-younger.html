小汉斯·霍尔拜因

Hans Holbein the Younger

1497–1543 · 瑞士

24 件作品 / works

小汉斯·霍尔拜因是德国画家，最擅长油画和版画，属于欧洲北方文艺复兴时代的艺术家，他最著名的作品是许多肖像画和系列木版画《死神之舞》。

Hans Holbein the Younger was a German painter and printmaker of Swiss descent who worked in a Northern Renaissance style, and is considered one of the greatest portraitists of the 16th century.
