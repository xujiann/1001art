胡安·米罗，西班牙加泰罗尼亚画家、雕塑家。以明快的色彩与富于童趣的生物形符号，构筑出充满诗意的超现实世界。

Joan Miró was a Catalan Spanish painter and sculptor whose bright colours and playful biomorphic signs create a poetic Surrealist world of his own.
