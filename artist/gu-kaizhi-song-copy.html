顾恺之（宋摹）

Gu Kaizhi (Song copy)

344–406 · 中国

1 件作品 / works

顾恺之，东晋画家、绘画理论家，被誉为「画绝」。传世《女史箴图》（今存宋摹本）以「以形写神」的笔法，成为中国人物画的典范。

Gu Kaizhi was an Eastern Jin painter and theorist revered as a founding master of Chinese figure painting; the Admonitions Scroll, surviving in a later Song copy, exemplifies his ideal of conveying spirit through form.
