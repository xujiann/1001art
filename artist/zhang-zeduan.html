张择端

Zhang Zeduan

1085–1145 · 中国

1 件作品 / works

张择端，北宋画家，以长卷《清明上河图》闻名。作品生动描绘北宋都城汴京的市井百态，是中国风俗画的巅峰之作。

Zhang Zeduan was a Northern Song painter famed for the handscroll Along the River During the Qingming Festival, a panoramic masterpiece of Chinese genre painting depicting daily life in the capital Bianjing.
