葛饰北斋

Katsushika Hokusai

1760–1849 · 日本

288 件作品 / works

葛饰北斋，日本江户时代浮世绘画家、版画家，自号「画狂人」。以木版画组画《富岳三十六景》（内含《神奈川冲浪里》）闻名于世，一生创作逾三万幅作品，其构图与色彩深刻影响了欧洲印象派与新艺术运动。

Katsushika Hokusai was a Japanese ukiyo-e painter and printmaker of the Edo period, best known for the woodblock series Thirty-Six Views of Mount Fuji, which includes the iconic Great Wave off Kanagawa. Extraordinarily prolific, his compositions and colour profoundly influenced European Impressionism and Art Nouveau.
