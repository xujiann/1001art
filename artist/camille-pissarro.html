卡米耶·毕沙罗

Camille Pissarro

1830–1903 · 法国

297 件作品 / works

卡米耶·毕沙罗，出生于丹属圣托马斯岛的法国印象派与新印象派画家。他是印象派运动的核心人物，也是唯一参加了全部八次印象派画展的画家；塞尚与高更皆尊其为师，以描绘乡村风景与农民劳作著称。

Camille Pissarro was a Danish-French Impressionist and Neo-Impressionist painter born on St Thomas. A central figure of the movement and the only artist to exhibit at all eight Impressionist shows, he was a mentor to Cézanne and Gauguin and is celebrated for his rural landscapes and scenes of peasant life.
