济安·贝尼尼

Gian Lorenzo Bernini

1598–1680 · 意大利

2 件作品 / works

济安·洛伦佐·贝尼尼，意大利巴洛克艺术的核心人物，雕塑家兼建筑家。代表作有《圣特蕾莎的沉迷》《阿波罗与达芙妮》，并主持设计了圣彼得大教堂广场的柱廊。

Gian Lorenzo Bernini was the leading figure of the Italian Baroque, a sculptor and architect whose masterpieces include the Ecstasy of Saint Teresa and Apollo and Daphne; he also designed the great colonnade of St Peter's Square.
