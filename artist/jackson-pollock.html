杰克逊·波洛克

Jackson Pollock

1912–1956 · 美国

2 件作品 / works

杰克逊·波洛克，美国抽象表现主义代表画家。以「滴画」技法在铺于地面的画布上泼洒、滴淌颜料，创造出充满能量的全幅式抽象绘画。

Jackson Pollock was a leading American Abstract Expressionist whose 'drip' technique—pouring and flinging paint onto canvas laid on the floor—produced energetic, all-over abstract paintings.
