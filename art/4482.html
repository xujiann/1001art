弗朗西斯科·戈雅创作的布面油画，约1815年，现藏于皇家圣费尔南多美术学院。

A Procession of Flagellants (Procesión de disciplinantes, or Procesión de flagelantes) is an oil-on-panel painting produced by Francisco de Goya between 1814 and 1816. In the foreground is a procession of Roman Catholic men dressed in white, wearing capirotes and whipping their bared backs in penitence.
