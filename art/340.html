《教宗依诺增爵十世像》是17世纪西班牙画家迪亚哥·委拉斯开兹的肖像画作品。1650年作。罗马，多利亚·潘菲利美术馆藏。

Portrait of Innocent X is a c. 1650 oil on canvas painting by Spanish artist Diego Velázquez, depicting Pope Innocent X, head of the Catholic Church from 1644 to 1655.
