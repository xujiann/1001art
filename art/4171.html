《凯风快晴》（日语：凯风快晴／がいふうかいせい，罗马化：Gaifū kaisei）是葛饰北斋的名所绘《富岳三十六景》之中的一幅，描绘的是富士山。

Fine Wind, Clear Morning (Japanese: 凱風快晴, Hepburn: Gaifū kaisei; literally South Wind, Clear Sky), also known as Red Fuji (赤富士, Akafuji), is a woodblock print by Japanese artist Hokusai (1760–1849), part of his Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji series, dating from c. 1830 to 1832.
