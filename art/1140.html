扬·范·艾克，1432年。早期尼德兰风格，现藏于wikibase未知值的概念。

The Just Judges, also called The Righteous Judges, is the lower left panel of the Ghent Altarpiece, painted by Jan van Eyck or his brother Hubert Van Eyck between 1430 and 1432.
