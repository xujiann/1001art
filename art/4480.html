弗朗西斯科·戈雅创作的木板油画，约1813年，现藏于伦敦国家美术馆。

The Portrait of the Duke of Wellington is an oil on panel painting by the Spanish artist Francisco de Goya of the British general Arthur Wellesley, 1st Duke of Wellington, during the latter's service in the Peninsular War. Goya painted three portraits of Wellington.
