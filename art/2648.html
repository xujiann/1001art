克劳德·莫奈创作的布面油画，约1918年，现藏于哥伦布艺术博物馆。

Weeping Willow is a 1918 oil painting by Claude Monet which depicts a weeping willow tree growing at the edge of his water garden pond in Giverny, France. It is exhibited at the Columbus Museum of Art in Columbus, Ohio. The painting is one of a series of Monet paintings of this weeping willow.
