安德烈亚·曼特尼亚，1456年。早期文艺复兴风格，现藏于卢浮宫。

The Crucifixion is a panel in the central part of the predella of a large altarpiece painted by Andrea Mantegna between 1457 and 1459 for the high altar of San Zeno, Verona .
