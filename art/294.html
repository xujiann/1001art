Madonna del Granduca

拉斐尔·圣齐奥 (Raphael)

年代 Date

馆藏 Collection

《大公爵圣母》是意大利文艺复兴艺术大师拉斐尔的一幅木板油画。这幅画大约绘制于1505 年，即拉斐尔抵达佛罗伦萨后不久。

The Madonna del Granduca is a Madonna painting by the Italian renaissance artist Raphael. It was probably painted in 1505, shortly after Raphael had arrived in Florence.
