威廉·霍尔曼·亨特，1873年。拉斐尔前派风格，现藏于曼彻斯特美术馆。

The Shadow of Death is a religious painting by the English painter William Holman Hunt, on which he worked from 1870 to 1873, during his second trip to the Holy Land.
