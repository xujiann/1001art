Léonard Renoir, The Artist's Father is an oil on canvas painting by the French artist Pierre-Auguste Renoir (1841–1919), created in the summer of 1869 at the age of 28. The painting depicts Renoir's father, a tailor, at 70 years old. Due to financial difficulties, Renoir was living with his parents at the time.
