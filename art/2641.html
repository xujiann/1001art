克劳德·莫奈创作的布面油画，约1884年，现藏于普希金造型艺术博物馆。

Haystack Near Giverny is an oil on canvas painting by Claude Monet, from 1884. It is held in the Pushkin Museum, in Moscow. It is a precursor to his 1890s Haystacks series. It was bought by Paul Durand-Ruel in 1906 and in spring 1907 he sold it on to Ivan Morozov.
