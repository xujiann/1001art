列奥纳多·达·芬奇，1503年。盛期文艺复兴风格，现藏于乌菲齐美术馆。

La Tavola Doria is a painting depicting the central part of the famous mural painting of the Battle of Anghiari by Leonardo Da Vinci, created between 1503 and 1505.
