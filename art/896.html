约翰·艾佛雷特·米莱，1858年。拉斐尔前派风格，现藏于伦敦国家美术馆。

The Vale of Rest is a painting by the English Pre-Raphaelite artist John Everett Millais. It depicts a twilight graveyard scene and prominently features two nuns.
