胡塞佩·德·里韦拉，1642年。巴洛克风格，现藏于卢浮宫。

The Clubfoot is a 1642 oil on canvas painting by Jusepe de Ribera. It is housed in the Musée du Louvre in Paris , and was painted in Naples.
