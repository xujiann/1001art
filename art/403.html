阿梅代奥·莫迪利亚尼，1918年。现代主义风格，现藏于国立丹麦美术馆。

Alice is a c.1918 oil on canvas painting by Amedeo Modigliani. It now hangs in the National Gallery of Denmark, in Copenhagen, to which it was donated in 1928.
