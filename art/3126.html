View of the Domaine Saint-Joseph (French: Vue du Domaine Saint-Joseph) is an oil painting on canvas by French artist Paul Cézanne. Another name given to the work is La Colline des pauvres ("The Poorhouse on The Hill"). Cézanne painted the work in the 1880s.
