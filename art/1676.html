亨利·马蒂斯，1908年。现代主义风格，现藏于埃尔米塔日博物馆。

Game of Bowls is a 1908 painting by the French artist Henri Matisse. The painting shows three young men, probably Matisse's sons and nephew, playing a game of boules.
