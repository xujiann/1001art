爱德华·马奈，1867年。19世纪绘画风格，现藏于奥赛博物馆。

Madame Manet at the Piano is a portrait by Édouard Manet of his wife Suzanne , painted in 1867–68 and now in the Musée d'Orsay, in Paris.
