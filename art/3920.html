Lorenzo Pagans and Auguste de Gas is the title of a painting by Edgar Degas. Painted in oil on canvas, it is 54.5 cm high and 39.5 cm wide. The double portrait, painted around 1871–1872, shows the Catalan tenor Lorenzo Pagans performing a song and guitar recital.
