《头戴蓝冠的圣母》是拉斐尔和他的学生詹弗朗切斯科·潘尼所作的一幅油画，完成年代很可能是1512年，地点则应是罗马。

The Madonna with the Blue Diadem is a painting by Raphael and his pupil Gianfrancesco Penni, and was most likely painted in Rome around 1510-1512, now at the Louvre.
