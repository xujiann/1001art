View from Rouelles is an 1858 painting by Claude Monet. The painting depicts the landscape surrounding a small stream, either the Rouelles or the Lézarde, in the Rouelles district of Le Havre in Normandy, France. Painted when Monet was seventeen, it is the earliest known painting by the artist.
