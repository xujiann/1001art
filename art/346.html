弹钢琴的少女是由艺术家皮埃尔-奥古斯特·勒努瓦于1892年所绘制，画风为印象派，现藏于奥赛美术馆。 这幅画代表了他晚年的画风。

Young Girls at the Piano is an oil-on-canvas painting by French artist Pierre-Auguste Renoir, a leading painter in the development of the Impressionist style.
