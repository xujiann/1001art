扬·斯滕，1665年。荷兰黄金时代风格，现藏于阿姆斯特丹国家博物馆。

The Feast of Saint Nicholas is an oil-on-canvas painting executed c. 1665–1668 by Dutch master Jan Steen, which is now in the Rijksmuseum in Amsterdam.
