卡米耶·毕沙罗创作的布面油画，约1897年，现藏于阿什莫林博物馆。

Bath Road, London is an 1897 Impressionist painting by the French artist Camille Pissarro with a scene of the new garden suburb of Bedford Park near Chiswick, noted for its distinctive Queen Anne Revival architecture.
