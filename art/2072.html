The Kingfisher is an oil on canvas painting by the Dutch artist Vincent van Gogh. It was painted from July to December 1886. The Kingfisher was painted in Paris, France. It is now located in Amsterdam, Netherlands, at the Van Gogh Museum. The Kingfisher shows a River Kingfisher in a marsh.
