皮耶-奥古斯特·雷诺瓦创作的布面油画，约1866年，现藏于瑞典国立博物馆。

Mother Anthony's Tavern (French: Le cabaret de la Mère Antony à Bourron-Marlotte), also known as At the Inn of Mother Anthony, is an 1866 oil-on-canvas painting made by French artist Pierre-Auguste Renoir during his Fontainebleau period.
