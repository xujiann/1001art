胡安·卢纳·德·圣佩德罗，1884年。学院派风格，现藏于菲律宾国家博物馆。

The Spoliarium is a painting by Filipino painter Juan Luna. Luna, working on canvas, spent eight months completing the painting which depicts dying gladiators.
