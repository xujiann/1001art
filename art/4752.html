Saint Sebastian is a painting of c. 1614 by Peter Paul Rubens, showing the Christian Saint Sebastian. It dates to the early years of Rubens's stay in Rome; its sinuous line and defined figures are thought to be the result of his studies of Michelangelo and of Flemish Mannerism.
