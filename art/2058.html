《忧愁》（荷兰语：Sorrow），或译为《忧伤》，是文森·梵高于1882年完成的一幅画作；两年后，他决定以艺术创作为其志业。画中人物是一位名叫Clasina Maria Hoornik（或称Sien）的32岁怀孕女性。该作品是梵高在其漫长又有时不确定的学徒时期的高潮之作。

Sorrow is a drawing by Vincent van Gogh, produced in 1882. The work, created two years after Van Gogh had decided to become an artist, depicts 32-year-old pregnant woman Clasina Maria Hoornik, familiarly known as Sien. The drawing is part of the Garman Ryan Collection held at The New Art Gallery Walsall.
