克劳德·莫奈创作的布面油画，约1895年，私人收藏。

The Mount Kolsaas series of oil paintings was created by the French Impressionist painter Claude Monet in 1895. In the winter of early 1895 Monet decided to undertake a painting trip to Norway, where his eldest stepson, Jacques Hoschedé, was living at the time.
