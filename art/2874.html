保罗·高更创作的布面油画，约1892年，现藏于普希金造型艺术博物馆。

Vairumati tei Oa is an 1892 painting by Paul Gauguin, produced during his time in Polynesia. Its title translates as Her name was Vairaumati. It remained in the artist's family before passing to Ambroise Vollard's gallery in Paris.
