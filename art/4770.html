彼得·保罗·鲁本斯创作的木板油画，约1601年，现藏于休士顿美术馆。

Leda and the Swan is an oil painting by Peter Paul Rubens, who painted two versions of this subject. The first was completed in 1601 and the second in 1602. Rubens was heavily influenced by Michelangelo, and both paintings are variations on Michelangelo's famous lost painting, which is known from copies and prints.
