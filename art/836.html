《百骏图》，又称《图成百骏》，是一幅全长 7.76 公尺的横幅手卷，内容描绘一百匹骏马。清代宫廷画家兼传教士郎世宁于 1728 年创作此画。

One Hundred Horses is a 7.76 meters wide Qing dynasty silk and ink painting by Giuseppe Castiglione, known in China by his adopted name Lang Shining .
