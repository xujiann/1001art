埃尔·格列柯，1570年。文艺复兴风格，现藏于卡波迪蒙特美术馆。

El Soplón or Boy Blowing on an Ember is a 1571-1572 oil on canvas painting by El Greco, almost certainly produced during his stay in Venice.
