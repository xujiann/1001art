阿尔布雷希特·丢勒创作的蚀刻版画，约1498年，现藏于博艾曼斯·范·伯宁恩博物馆。

The Sea Monster (German: Das Meerwunder) is a c. 1498–1500 copper engraving by the German Renaissance master Albrecht Dürer. It shows a voluptuous naked woman riding on the back of a merman, a male creature who is half-man, half-fish. The man wears a beard and antlers, while his lower body is covered in scales.
