阿尔布雷希特·丢勒创作的纸本素描，约1498年，现藏于国家艺廊。

Saint Sebastian at the Column is a 1500 copper engraving by the German artist Albrecht Dürer (1471–1528), part of his series of the Lives of the Saints. Dürer intended the work to represent both an idealised vision of 15th-century beauty, and an homage to classical sculpture.
