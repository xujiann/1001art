彼得·保罗·鲁本斯创作的布面油画，约1617年，现藏于维也纳艺术史博物馆。

Theodosius and Saint Ambrose is a painting by Peter Paul Rubens, with assistance from his main pupil Anthony van Dyck, executed c. 1615–1616. It is now in the Kunsthistorisches Museum in Vienna.
