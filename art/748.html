约瑟夫·赖特，1772年。洛可可风格，现藏于德比博物馆与艺术画廊。

The Captive King is a sketch by Joseph Wright of Derby completed in 1772 or 1773. It depicts the French nobleman Guy de Lusignan held prisoner by Saladin.
