Shepherdess with her Flock

年代 Date
1863
媒材 Medium
布面油画 / Oil on canvas
馆藏 Collection

地区 Region
法国 / France

《牧羊女与羊群》（Shepherdess with her Flock）是法国巴比松派画家让-弗朗索瓦·米勒创作的一幅布面油画，创作于1863年左右，现藏于巴黎奥赛美术馆。

Shepherdess with her Flock is an oil-on-canvas painting by Jean-François Millet, created c. 1863. It is held in the Musee d'Orsay, in Paris.
