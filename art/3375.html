伦勃朗创作的布面油画，约1647年，现藏于弗里克收藏。

The Hundred Guilder Print is an etching with drypoint by Rembrandt, measuring 278 x 388 mm (platemark). The etching's popular name derives from the large sum of money supposedly charged for it.
