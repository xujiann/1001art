彼得·保罗·鲁本斯创作的布面油画，约1613年，现藏于德累斯顿国家艺术收藏馆。

Drunken Hercules is an oil-on-canvas painting by an unknown artist, executed around 1700 after Peter Paul Rubens's Drunken Hercules Supported by a Pair of Satyrs. It is in the collection of the Odesa Museum of Western and Eastern Art.
