The Louvre, Foggy Morning (French: Le Louvre, matin brumeux) is an oil-on-canvas painting by the Danish-French Impressionist painter Camille Pissarro. Painted in February 1901, the work is in the collection of the Bank of the Republic, the central bank of Colombia, and currently displayed at the Museo Botero in Bogotá.
