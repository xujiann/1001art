弗朗西斯科·戈雅，1788年。新古典与浪漫风格，现藏于普拉多博物馆。

Blind Man's Bluff is one of the Rococo oil-on-linen cartoons produced by the Spanish artist Francisco de Goya for tapestries for the Royal Palace of El Pardo.
