彼得·保罗·鲁本斯创作的布面油画，约1606年，现藏于国家名胜古迹信托。

Portrait of a Noblewoman with an Attendant is an oil-on-canvas portrait by Peter Paul Rubens, executed circa 1606. The painting has been thought to depict Maria Grimaldi, daughter of Carlo Grimaldi, a marquess who in 1607 loaned his villa at Sampadierna to Rubens and his patron Vincenzo I Gonzaga of Mantua.
