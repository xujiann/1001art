保罗·塞尚，1873年。19世纪绘画风格，现藏于奥赛博物馆。

A Modern Olympia is a c. 1873–74 painting by Impressionist painter Paul Cézanne. The painting's title and subject are a homage to Édouard Manet's 1863 painting Olympia.
