《凝望月亮的两人》是德国浪漫主义画家卡斯帕·大卫·弗雷德里希的一系列相似作品，该系列同时也是他最为有名的作品。

Two Men Contemplating the Moon and Man and Woman Contemplating the Moon are a series of similar paintings by Caspar David Friedrich, the setting being among his best-known works.
