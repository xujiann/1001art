《杜比尼花园》（荷兰语：De tuin van Daubigny），或译多比尼花园，是梵高绘制的三幅同名、同主题（即夏尔-弗朗索瓦·多比尼的花园）画作，现分别收藏于巴塞尔美术馆、广岛美术馆和梵高博物馆。这三幅作品完成于1890年五月到七月间的瓦兹河畔欧韦，是梵高一生绘制的最后几幅作品。

Daubigny's Garden, painted three times by Vincent van Gogh, depicts the enclosed garden of Charles-François Daubigny, a painter whom Van Gogh admired throughout his life. Van Gogh started with a small study of a section of the garden. Then he worked on two double-square paintings of the full walled garden.
